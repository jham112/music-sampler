Kim Hae-Sol, under the stage name of Zion.T, is a famous hip hop and R & B artist in South Korea. He is also known as a singer-songwriter, meaning he writes his own songs. He debuted in 2011 with his first single, "Click Me" featuring another hip hop artist Dok2. Zion.T rose to popularity with his digital single "Yanghwa BRDG". This song showed his personal side and resonated with listeners. With the "Eat" song, he won an award for the Best Vocal Performance (Male) at the 17th Mnet Asian Music Awards.
